Introduction

From the moment I dialed-up at the age of 12, I was intrigued by the online world. After creating my first website in raw HTML, I was hooked.

In these 17 years, I've touched nearly every piece that makes the web work. From designing in Photoshop, mockups in cross-browser HTML/CSS (oh the days of IE5.5 compatibility), progressive enhancement with Javascript, writing a backend application through to setting up and running servers, DNS hosting, optimizing for search engines, and even buying and selling domain names.

I love full-stack development and am particularly fond of Javascript, especially since Ryan Dahl's original Node.js demo in 2009. I'm heavily invested in the user experience and always have end users in mind. And ultimately, I like to build cool things with cool people.
